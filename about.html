Allow me

to introduce myself


My name is Cynthia Hutauruk and I am a Denver-based UI Software Engineer specializing in the MERN stack. I'm tenacious, naturally inquisitive and passionate about advancing narratives through technology.

My journey first began in middle school Computer Club where I created tesselations, Super Mario emulators, and wrote out script commands on Telnet/MUD clients to play text-based RPG multi-user dungeon and dragon games. In high school, I created Flash videos, basic web design on Dreamweaver/Adobe Photoshop, and played with SQL databases. I pursued a Psychology major specializing in research and switched my minor from French to Computer Information Systems. After a decade of working in healthcare, I decided to become a self-taught programmer before I found my current bootcamp program.

I have acquired time and project management skills, an eye for detail while auditing data, and enjoy problem-solving amidst collaboring with others on team projects.


I'm always open to opportunities.
